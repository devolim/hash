Delete your Hash account

Last updated: February 2026

Warning: Deleting your Hash account is immediate and irreversible. All your data (messages, contacts, encryption keys) will be permanently erased. There is no way to recover your account after deletion.

Delete your account from the app

The simplest way to delete your account is to use the Delete my account option directly in the app:

  1. Open the Hash app on your device
  2. Go to Settings by tapping the gear icon
  3. Scroll down to the "Account" section
  4. Tap "Delete my account"
  5. Confirm the deletion by following the on-screen instructions

What happens? As soon as you confirm, all your data is instantly erased, both on your device and on our servers. The app returns to its initial state, as if it had just been installed.

Data deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently erased:

✕ Your Hash ID
✕ Public encryption keys
✕ Private keys (on the device)
✕ Device information
✕ Unread messages in transit
✕ All contacts
✕ Conversation history
✕ Local files and media

Alternative method: by email

Can't access the app anymore?

If you no longer have access to your device or the app, you can request account deletion by writing to us at:

hash-support@devolim.fr

Please include in your email:

  • Your Hash ID (format 000-000-XXX) if you know it
  • Any information that can help identify your account

We will process your request within 48 hours.

Consequences of deletion

After your account is deleted:

  • Your contacts will no longer be able to send you messages. Messages sent to your former account will be rejected
  • Your Hash ID will be released and may potentially be reassigned to another user
  • Your local conversations will be erased from your device
  • Messages you had sent that were already received by your contacts will remain on their devices (we have no control over data stored locally by other users)
  • It is impossible to recover your account or your data after deletion

Contact

For any questions about deleting your account, contact us:

  • Email: hash-support@devolim.fr
  • Privacy: hash-privacy@devolim.fr
