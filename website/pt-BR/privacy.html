Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

In summary: Hash is designed to collect virtually no personal data. Your messages are end-to-end encrypted, we cannot read them. No analytics, no advertising, no sharing of data with third parties.

2. Data collected

Hash was designed following the principle of data minimization. We only collect the strict minimum necessary for the service to function:

2.1 Data we collect

  • Hash ID: a randomly generated unique identifier (format 000-000-XXX), not linked to your real identity
  • Device identifier: a unique technical identifier used to associate your account with your device
  • Platform: the type of device used (iOS or Android), necessary for sending notifications
  • Public encryption keys: the public keys from your Signal Protocol keychain, necessary for your contacts to send you encrypted messages
  • Mailbox token: a cryptographically derived token from your keys, used for anonymous message reception

2.2 Data we do NOT collect

  • Message content: all messages are end-to-end encrypted (E2E). We cannot read them, even if we wanted to
  • Contact list: your address book stays on your device, we never access it
  • Location: geolocation data is only transmitted when you voluntarily send a location message. It is end-to-end encrypted and cannot be read or stored by our servers
  • Email address: not required, not collected
  • Phone number: not required, not collected
  • IP address: not stored in our databases
  • Communication metadata: we don't know who communicates with whom thanks to the anonymous token system
  • Photos, files, media: all multimedia content is encrypted client-side before sending and only passes through our servers as unreadable encrypted data

3. Encryption and security

Hash uses state-of-the-art encryption technologies to protect your communications:

  • Signal Protocol: end-to-end encryption protocol also used by Signal and WhatsApp, providing forward secrecy and future secrecy
  • AES-256: military-grade symmetric encryption algorithm for message and file encryption
  • Curve25519: elliptic curve for Diffie-Hellman key exchange
  • Double Ratchet: each message uses a unique session key, automatically renewed
  • Sealed Sender: anonymous sender technology preventing the server from knowing the sender of a message

Your private keys never leave your device. They are stored locally in encrypted storage (Hive) and are never transmitted to our servers.

4. Data retention period

  • Messages in transit: stored on the server for a maximum of 24 hours. As soon as the recipient confirms receipt, the message is immediately and irreversibly deleted from the server
  • Account data: retained as long as the account is active. Immediately and permanently deleted upon account deletion (panic button or manual deletion)
  • Files and media: same policy as messages, deleted upon confirmed receipt

5. Local storage

Local data (decrypted messages, contacts, keys) is stored only on your device in an encrypted Hive database. This data is never synced to our servers or to the cloud.

In case of loss or change of device, this data is permanently lost. This is a deliberate design choice: one device = one account, no recovery possible.

6. Sharing data with third parties

No data sharing. Hash does not use any analytics tools, any advertising, or any tracker. We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to any third parties whatsoever.

Our servers are hosted by Supabase. The data stored on these servers is either unreadable encrypted data (messages) or minimal technical data (Hash ID, public keys).

Push notifications are delivered via Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google LLC). Only the FCM device token is processed by Google for this purpose. No message content is transmitted through this service. Firebase Privacy Policy.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: you can request what data we hold about you (Hash ID, public keys, device identifier)
  • Right of rectification: you can request the correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: you can delete all your data at any time via the panic button in the app, or via the Settings section. Deletion is immediate and irreversible
  • Right to portability: given the minimal volume of data collected, you can contact us to obtain a copy of your data
  • Right to object: you can object to the processing of your data by deleting your account
  • Right to lodge a complaint: you can file a complaint with the CNIL (www.cnil.fr)

To exercise your rights, contact us at hash-privacy@devolim.fr.

8. Your Rights (US Privacy Laws)

If you are a resident of California (CCPA/CPRA), Virginia (VCDPA), Colorado (CPA), or another US state with privacy legislation, you have the following rights:

  • Right to know: you may request information about the personal data we collect and how it is used
  • Right to delete: you may request the deletion of your personal data at any time via the in-app deletion feature or by contacting us
  • Right to opt-out of sale: we do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration
  • Right to non-discrimination: we will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, use the in-app account deletion feature or contact us at hash-privacy@devolim.fr.

9. Minimum age

Hash is intended for persons aged 13 or older. In certain European Union countries where local legislation requires it, the minimum age may be 16.

We do not knowingly collect data concerning children below the applicable minimum age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child is using Hash without your consent, contact us at hash-privacy@devolim.fr.

10. Cookies and tracking technologies

The Hash app does not use any cookies, any tracking pixels, or any analytics SDK. The website hash.devolim.fr also does not use any tracking cookies.

11. International transfers

Minimal technical data (Hash ID, public keys) may be processed on servers located outside the European Economic Area. However, given that all sensitive data is end-to-end encrypted and we do not collect any personally identifiable data, the risk to your privacy is minimal.

12. Data security

We implement the following security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption of all communications (Signal Protocol)
  • AES-256 encryption of local data
  • Authentication via PIN code and biometrics
  • Destruction code (duress PIN) for emergency erasure
  • Screenshot protection (FLAG_SECURE on Android)
  • Automatic deletion of messages from the server after delivery
  • Database access exclusively through secure functions (security definer)
  • RLS (Row Level Security) policies on all tables

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. In case of a substantial change, we will notify you via a notification in the app. The date of the last update is indicated at the top of this page.
